About

By Maya Camilleri | 4 December 2004

I am an 18 year old. Personally I am a creative person with big dreams, love to experiment and developing new skills. Also I am an active person, it’s extremely important to me to stay healthy and be fit. Football, is the sports I participate in. I always plan my work and focus on one task at a time.Being organized is one of the most things I adore being in everything. I love Art. Art makes me express my feelings in my drawings. My favorite media is acrylic and watercolours, but I like to experiment with different media.











Short story: I’m an Interactive Media student who has been experimenting with art and Media my whole life. I spend my free time making digital paintings and sketches, mostly character Design. As for the long story, I’ve been drawing pretty much my whole life. Ever since I can remember, my teachers and classmates told me that drawing was my ‘talent,’ which motivated me to keep practicing and focus on that era. I live in Malta. I have been to an exebithion, where I have presented "imhabbti infinita tfur bl-isbah fjuri" painting on canvas with acrylic.
